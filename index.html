Crystal Collector

  • You will be given a random Number to Guess at the start of the game.
  • By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.
  • You win the game by matching your Total Score to the random Number to Guess.
  • You lose the game if your Total Score goes above the Number to Guess.
  • The value for each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
